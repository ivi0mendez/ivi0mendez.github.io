What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a frenetic, competitive online game that fuses the speed and intensity of real-time strategy (ETR) with elements of RPGs. Two teams of powerful champions, each with a unique design and game style, compete face-to-face across various battlefields and game modes. With an ever-expanding roster of champions, frequent updates and an exciting competitive landscape, League of Legends offers unlimited gaming possibilities for users of all skill levels.

HOW TO PLAY?

Champions:

The League has several archetypes of champions, from evil criminal minds to monstrous and epic creatures, going through all sorts of characters you can imagine. The different champions cover different positions and strategies, so do not be discouraged if your first choice does not fit your style of play.

Rails

There are three ways that connect your base with that of the enemy. These paths are called lanes and serve to confront the enemy team. To conquer you will have to advance the lane to the enemy base and destroy the nexus that is in the center of its base.
